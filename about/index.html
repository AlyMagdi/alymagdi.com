I'm Aly AKA Rony, a dedicated security researcher with a passion for uncovering vulnerabilities in some of the world's largest technology platforms. With experience working through HackerOne and BugCrowd, I've developed expertise in web and application security.

Since starting my journey in cybersecurity in 2024, I have successfully identified and responsibly reported 200+ valid security vulnerabilities across web applications, APIs, and infrastructure components. Each report has contributed to strengthening the defenses of global organizations.

I believe in responsible disclosure and in empowering companies to improve their security posture. Beyond hunting for bugs, I actively research new attack vectors, contribute to the security community, and stay updated with the latest developments in cybersecurity.
